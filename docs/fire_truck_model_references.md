# Fire Truck 3D Modeling References

These references were used to shape the WebGL truck model proportions and compartment hotspot placement.

## FDNY Photo References

- FDNY engine company gallery (example apparatus pages): [FDNYTrucks.com](https://www.fdnytrucks.com/)
- Engine company photo index: [FDNY Engines](https://www.fdnytrucks.com/files/html/staten/e260.htm)
- Ladder company photo index: [FDNY Ladders](https://www.fdnytrucks.com/files/html/queens/l154.htm)
- Wikimedia Commons FDNY fire engine media: [Category: Fire engines of the FDNY](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Fire_engines_of_the_FDNY)
- Wikimedia Commons FDNY ladder truck media: [Category: Turntable ladder trucks of the FDNY](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Turntable_ladder_trucks_of_the_FDNY)

## Apparatus Layout / Equipment Placement

- Pump panel and hose storage overview: [FireRescue1 - Engine Company Equipment](https://www.firerescue1.com/fire-products/pump-panels/)
- Pumper configuration and compartments (manufacturer context): [Pierce Pumper Apparatus](https://www.piercemfg.com/fire-trucks/pumpers)
- Engine and ladder operational context: [U.S. Fire Administration resources](https://www.usfa.fema.gov/)

## Modeling Notes Applied in Prototype

- Engine hotspots: hose bed (rear), pump panel (side), cab door.
- Ladder hotspots: aerial base controls, rear tool compartment, cab door.
- Ambulance hotspots: rear medical compartment and side door.
- Visual proportions are intentionally gameplay-first and will be replaced by detailed mesh assets in next pass.
